Personal Interests

Japanese Culture

I have a great interest in Japanese culture that was sparked in my childhood by watching Anime shows, and also because I have Japanese roots in my family. In February 2020, I finally had access to a Japanese course at my university, and since then, I've been studying the language. In the spring semester of 2021, I co-founded my university's first Japanese Culture Club. We worked to showcase Japanese culture to the general public in my hometown San Luis Potosi, Mexico, to strengthen the relationship between both cultures. In addition, I hosted a webinar series where we invited people with a strong relationship with Japan. This journey has introduced me to new interests such as cuisine and history. Although the Japanese Culture Club project has already concluded, please check our Facebook page if you are interested in our created content. Also, check our YouTube channel to see the webinar series.

Esports

I started playing League of Legends, a very popular MOBA videogame, in spring 2017, getting very engaged with the competitive nature of the game and its high level of strategy. I was fortunate that my university fomented Esports competition on its campus. Therefore, I had the opportunity to be the captain of a League of Legends team at my university from August 2020 to June 2021, winning the campus tournament twice. Furthermore, I participated in national tournaments such as the Borregos Esports Cup and College National Championship in Mexico.
